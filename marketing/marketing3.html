What's covered

Ten sections. Every angle.

From your company name to what happens if things fall apart. Cherrytree covers everything your founding team needs to address and explains why each section matters.

Company name, incorporation state, business purpose, and founding mission. Sets the legal foundation for everything that follows.
Roles, responsibilities, titles, and contact details for each cofounder. Establishes who's who from day one.
How ownership is divided between cofounders and why. The most important number in your company, written down clearly.
Cliff period, vesting timeline, acceleration triggers, and what happens to unvested equity when a cofounder leaves.
Voting rights, which decisions require full agreement, and how deadlocks get resolved before they become disputes.
Who owns what was built before and after the company formed. Prior inventions, assignment clauses, and work-for-hire.
Salaries, deferred pay, and the spending limit before a cofounder needs to check with the rest of the team.
What happens when someone isn't performing, or decides to leave. Cure periods, termination causes, and notice periods.
Confidentiality obligations, non-compete duration, and non-solicitation terms to protect the company after a departure.
Governing law, dispute resolution method, amendment process, and the legal framework for the entire agreement.
